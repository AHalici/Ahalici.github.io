Page 2: About Emirates Stadium

The Emirates Stadium is the home ground of Arsenal Football Club. Located in Islington, North London, it officially opened on 22 July 2006. With a seating capacity of over 60,000, it stands as one of the largest football stadiums in England. Known for its modern design and state-of-the-art facilities, the Emirates Stadium provides a vibrant atmosphere for Arsenal's matches, attracting fans from around the world.

Throughout its illustrious history, Arsenal Football Club has clinched numerous trophies and accolades. From domestic league titles to FA Cup triumphs, the Gunners have left an indelible mark on English football. Notable achievements include winning the English Premier League 13 times and the FA Cup a record 14 times.
